# Welcome to Dino-Man! 🦖

This repository is a tribute to the magnificent creatures that once roamed our Earth: **Dinosaurs**!

## What are Dinosaurs?
Dinosaurs were a diverse group of reptiles of the clade Dinosauria. They first appeared during the Triassic period, approximately 231 million years ago, and were the dominant terrestrial vertebrates for 135 million years, from the start of the Jurassic (about 201 million years ago) until the end of the Cretaceous (66 million years ago), when the Cretaceous–Paleogene extinction event led to the extinction of most dinosaur groups.

## Fun Facts About Dinosaurs
*   The word "dinosaur" comes from the Greek words "deinos" (terrible) and "sauros" (lizard).
*   Not all dinosaurs were huge; some were as small as chickens.
*   Birds are the only surviving lineage of dinosaurs.
*   The Tyrannosaurus Rex had a bite force stronger than any other land animal.
